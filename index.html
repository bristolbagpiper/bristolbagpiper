Why hire julian?

Experience

Being a piper since his early teens, Julian has amassed a wealth of experience over the years by piping weddings, funerals, and parties for decades.

Versatility

Throughout his playing career, Julian has built a large repertoire of tunes, from piobaireachd to all the traditional favourites. See the Audio page for more information.

Professionalism

Joining the Scots Guards at the age of 17, Julian has performed countless official duties such as the Trooping of the Colour in front of the Royal Family, as well as playing for foreign dignitaries, and many other members of the top brass.

The band

Julian has played with the City of Bristol Pipes and Drums as pipe major for 20 years. Over this time, he has proved to be reliable to a fault and will assuredly carry over these skills to your special occasion.
